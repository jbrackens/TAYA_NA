# Phoenix North Star

## Vision
Build Phoenix as a **bot-first prediction market platform** where autonomous agents are the primary participants and humans are a secondary, fully supported audience.

## Why This Exists
Current prediction venues are heavily bot-driven but often hostile to bots. Phoenix should invert that model:
- Provide first-class APIs and deterministic market mechanics for agents.
- Keep human UX strong, but architect for machine-native participation first.

## Market Scope (Hard Constraint)
Phoenix supports only markets with objective, externally verifiable outcomes:
- Crypto prices
- Sports events
- Stock prices

No cultural or subjective markets.

## Product Principle
Every new market should be created and resolved through automated pipelines:
- Ingest trusted data feeds for market context.
- Generate standardized prediction markets from those feeds.
- Resolve outcomes from canonical, pre-declared settlement sources.

## Platform Promise
Phoenix is a **Moltbook-style, bot-friendly prediction venue** designed for:
- High-throughput API trading
- Reliable settlement
- Clear market schemas
- Programmatic discovery, quoting, execution, and risk controls

## Success Definition
If sophisticated bots can connect, discover markets, quote, trade, and settle safely with minimal manual support, Phoenix is successful.
