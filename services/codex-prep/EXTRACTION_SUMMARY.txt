Models: Wallet, WalletLine, CommissionConfig, ExternalOrder, PartnerTransactionApiKeys

Events: Publishes WALLET_NEW_LINE events to Kafka

REFERRAL SERVICE (rmx-referral-microservice)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Purpose: Manage referral tree and bonus distribution
Models: ReferralUserTree (hierarchical), ReferralHistoryModel, PartnerConfiguration
API: 5 endpoints for referral status, links, history
Events: Consumes WALLET_NEW_LINE, publishes REFERRAL_BONUS
Workflow: Listen for wallet events → Calculate bonuses → Create commission lines

──────────────────────────────────

Models: ProcessModel, ProcessStepModel, ProcessLogModel, ProcessConstantsModel
Pattern: State machine with step-by-step execution and audit logging
Integration: Publishes events, triggers wallet updates, integrates with Cameleon
